ABOUT THIS DOC

This doc will briefly introduce myself, my role in the company, what I value most and how to reach me.

MY ROLE

I'm a software developer that loves to see how a piece of code translates to a client satisfaction.

I take pride on the small tools that make my life easier and on automating the tedious tasks.

WHAT I VALUE

Simplicity Simpler things describe the world better

Proactiveness Bring me a problem and we can work together in a solution, but a solution is even more welcome.

Freedom I care about results, I wont judge how they were achieved.

Curiosity Break it to learn from it.

Critical Thought I strongly believe that you can learn more from mistakes than success.

Humility We are all just humans. Unless you are an AI.

Humor I take my work very seriously but I'm also having a lot of fun, both can coexist together.

HOW TO REACH ME

I’m a very accessible person all the time, I prefer async communication when possible, but live interaction is also an option. You can contact me by:

Phone call Rarely happens and my children might be using it, but still an acceptable method.

Slack You can expect me to reply you back quickly, unless it’s way off my hours and I’m sleeping.

E-mail I prefer email for discussions that’s better keep a paper trail.

Video call Still acceptable, but I’m of the opinion that it consumes a lot of our time, usually an email or a few lines of chat is better.

IRC Not so much these days, and I miss them.
